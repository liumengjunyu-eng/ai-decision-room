# 🧠 AI Decision OS

> **The Operating System for Human Decisions in the Age of Multiple AIs.**
> A system that converts multi-AI conflicting opinions into structured, executable human decisions.

---

## 🚨 Problem

Users face ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek — and get **conflicting answers**.
They don't lack information. They lack **decision confidence**.

> Today's problem is not intelligence.  
> It is **decision overload under conflicting intelligence.**

---

## 💡 Solution

**AI Decision OS** turns conflicting AI opinions into a structured, explainable decision report.

Instead of asking *"What is the answer?"*, we answer:  
*"What do all AIs think, where do they disagree, and what should I do next?"*

- Semantic Consistency (20%)
- Counterfactual Robustness (20%)

### Conflict Engine
- TF-IDF cosine similarity
- Opposition detection
- Conflict matrix construction

### Risk Forecast
- 3-path outcome simulation
- Success probability calculation
- Counterfactual analysis
